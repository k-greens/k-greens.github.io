Features





  • No Open Drains minimising harmful insects (flies, cockroaches, mosquitoes etc.), rodent stench and diseases.
  • Gated, fenced and walled with ample parking , walking trails and play area for security, safety and well being of residents and tenants.
  • Metered utilities for greatly minimizing and personalizing maintenance cost.
  • Living space ergonomically designed by architect with space for living, dining and comfort.
